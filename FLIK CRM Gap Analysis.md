# FLIK CRM Gap Analysis

**Author:** VP of Product (AI Persona)  
**Target Audience:** Engineering, Product, and Design Teams  
**Date:** March 10, 2026

## Executive Summary
For a shipping aggregator, the "CRM" (Customer Relationship Management) layer is the bridge between logistics operations and customer retention. It transforms a transactional shipping tool into a growth platform. This analysis focuses on how FLIK can leverage shipping data to help merchants manage their customer relationships more effectively.

---

## 1. The Role of CRM in Logistics
In a shipping-first ecosystem, a CRM should handle:
1.  **Customer Profiles**: Centralizing shipping history, preferences, and lifetime value (LTV).
2.  **Communication**: Automated tracking updates, delivery notifications, and feedback loops.
3.  **Retention**: Identifying "at-risk" customers (due to late deliveries) or "loyal" customers (high frequency).
4.  **Support**: Providing merchants with the data needed to answer "Where is my order?" (WISMO) instantly.

---

## 2. Competitive UX Benchmarks
To lead the market, FLIK's CRM capabilities should be compared against:
*   **Klaviyo (Post-Purchase)**: Excellence in segmenting customers based on purchase and shipping behavior.
*   **AfterShip**: The gold standard for branded tracking pages and proactive delivery notifications.
*   **Gorgias**: Deep integration of shipping data into customer support helpdesks.

---

## 3. Anticipated Product Gaps
*   **Siloed Data**: Shipping data (AWB, Status) is often disconnected from Customer data (Name, Phone). Merchants cannot easily see "How many orders did Gibran place this month?"
*   **Lack of Proactive Communication**: Merchants currently rely on the courier's SMS/Email. A best-in-class CRM would allow the *merchant* to send branded updates via WhatsApp.
*   **Segmented Analytics**: No ability to filter customers by shipping region or "COD Return Rate" (crucial for Indonesian merchants to identify high-risk buyers).
*   **Branded Tracking Experience**: Customers are often sent to the courier's (JNE/J&T) generic website. This is a lost opportunity for merchant branding and upsells.

---

## 4. Strategic Recommendations
1.  **Unified Customer View**: Create a "Shopper Profile" that aggregates all Invoices and Shipments associated with a single phone number.
2.  **WhatsApp Notification Engine**: Enable merchants to trigger automated "Out for Delivery" or "Delivered" messages via their own FLIK-powered WhatsApp API.
3.  **High-Risk Buyer Flagging**: Use cross-merchant data to flag customers with a high "COD Rejection" history.
4.  **Branded Tracking Pages**: Replace generic courier links with a FLIK-hosted page featuring the merchant's logo, social media links, and "Recommended Products."
